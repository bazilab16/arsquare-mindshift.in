Hey There!

Welcome to our online patient registration portal, "One4Patient"! We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. As part of our compliance with HIPAA regulations, we have implemented a secure and convenient way for you to access and manage your medical records.

To complete your registration process, we will redirect you to our HIPAA-compliant portal, "One4Patient." Once registered, you can access your medical records, test results, and treatment plans. In addition, you can conveniently communicate with your healthcare professionals through this secure platform.

To ensure the confidentiality of your information, we will share your login credentials via email and text message to the mobile number provided during registration. We understand the importance of safeguarding your data and have taken necessary measures to protect it.

If you have any concerns regarding the security of email communication, please let us know, and we will make alternative arrangements to suit your preferences. Your peace of mind is our priority.

Thank you for choosing our online patient registration portal. We look forward to providing you with exceptional care and a seamless experience.

About Company

An initiative to be a pioneer in an education IT

Welcome to MindShift, the comprehensive mental health monitoring tool Education2.0's SchoolSense+ LMS/LXP provides. At MindShift, we understand that students' mental well-being is as important as their academic success. We have partnered with top-quality psychologists to offer specialized services and support to address various mental health issues.

Our team of experts is dedicated to helping students overcome anxiety disorders, providing depression therapy, and facilitating group therapy sessions. We believe in a holistic approach that considers each student's unique needs and challenges. Integrating MindShift with the powerful One4patient EMR/EHR/LIS system ensures that students receive personalized and effective care.

With MindShift, Education2.0 is committed to nurturing the mental well-being of students, empowering them to thrive academically and reach their full potential. We aim to create a safe and supportive environment where students can openly discuss their mental health concerns and receive the care they deserve.

Please explore our website to learn more about the services and resources provided by MindShift. Together, we can positively impact students' lives, ensuring they have the tools and support they need to succeed in their educational journey.

Welcome to Education2.0, where we believe in the power of education and holistic well-being. As the team behind ArSquare Development Pvt. Ltd., we bring you SchoolSense+ LMS/LXP "MindShift," an innovative initiative designed to prioritize student mental health alongside academic performance.

With a collective experience of 18-20 years in Education IT and Healthcare IT, our dedicated team understands the crucial link between mental health and academic success. We are passionate about creating a nurturing environment where students can thrive both academically and emotionally.

At Education2.0, we recognize that students face various challenges that can impact their mental well-being. That's why we have developed MindShift, a comprehensive solution that combines cutting-edge technology with evidence-based therapeutic approaches.

Our team of experts has carefully curated a range of resources and tools that address the unique mental health needs of students. From anxiety to substance abuse, ADHD to trauma, and perfectionism to stress, we are committed to providing tailored support to help students overcome these challenges.

Through case studies and research, we have identified several therapeutic interventions that have proven to be effective in supporting students' mental health. Exposure Therapy, Motivational Interviewing, Behavioral Therapy, Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) are just a few examples of the approaches we incorporate into our programs.

With MindShift, we aim to foster resilience, emotional intelligence, and overall well-being among students. By equipping them with the necessary tools and strategies, we empower students to navigate the complexities of life while achieving their academic goals.

At Education2.0, we firmly believe that investing in student mental health is an investment in their future. Join us on this transformative journey as we revolutionize education and prioritize the well-being of every student.

Together, let's create a brighter, healthier, and more successful tomorrow for our students.

Mission:

Our mission is to prioritize student mental health in India by raising awareness, reducing stigma, and providing accessible and affordable mental health services. We aim to create a supportive school environment where students feel comfortable talking about their mental healthand receive the necessary support.

Vision:

We envision a future where student mental health is valued and prioritized in India. We strive for a society where all students have access to the mental health resources they need, leading to improved academic performance, overall well-being, and reduced stigma surrounding mental health.

  • Empathy:

    We believe in understanding and empathizing with the unique mental health challenges faced by students in India. We strive to provide compassionate care and support to promote their well-being.

  • Collaboration:

    We value collaboration among students, parents, teachers, and mental health professionals to create a comprehensive support system for student mental health.

  • Accessibility:

    We are committed to making mental health services accessible and affordable for all students in India, regardless of their background or financial capabilities.

  • Education:

    We emphasize the importance of education and awareness about mental health issues to reduce stigma and promote a culture of understanding and empathy.

  • Inclusivity:

    We recognize the importance of inclusivity and aim to create a safe and supportive environment where students from all backgrounds can thrive mentally, emotionally, and academically.
