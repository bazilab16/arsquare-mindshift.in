Hey There!

Welcome to our online patient registration portal, "One4Patient"! We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. As part of our compliance with HIPAA regulations, we have implemented a secure and convenient way for you to access and manage your medical records.

To complete your registration process, we will redirect you to our HIPAA-compliant portal, "One4Patient." Once registered, you can access your medical records, test results, and treatment plans. In addition, you can conveniently communicate with your healthcare professionals through this secure platform.

To ensure the confidentiality of your information, we will share your login credentials via email and text message to the mobile number provided during registration. We understand the importance of safeguarding your data and have taken necessary measures to protect it.

If you have any concerns regarding the security of email communication, please let us know, and we will make alternative arrangements to suit your preferences. Your peace of mind is our priority.

Thank you for choosing our online patient registration portal. We look forward to providing you with exceptional care and a seamless experience.

Education2.0's SchoolSense+: Prioritizing Mental Health with MindShift

  • Education2.0's SchoolSense+ LMS/LXP is a revolutionary educational platform beyond traditional learning management systems. It understands that students' mental health is crucial to their overall well-being and academic success. To address this, SchoolSense+ includes a robust mental health monitoring tool called "MindShift."

  • MindShift: Monitoring Mental Health

    MindShift is an innovative feature integrated into SchoolSense+ that focuses on identifying and addressing common mental health issues among students. It recognizes that student learning disabilities and anxiety disorders are prevalent challenges that can significantly impact academic performance and overall quality of life.

  • Understanding Student Learning Disabilities

    Student learning disabilities are a common mental health issue that affects many students. These disabilities can manifest in various forms, such as dyslexia, dyscalculia, dysgraphia, or attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD). Recognizing the signs of learning disabilities is essential to provide appropriate support and interventions.

  • Addressing Anxiety Disorders

    Anxiety disorders are another significant mental health concern among students. Anxiety can manifest in different ways, including excessive worry, panic attacks, social anxiety, or generalized anxiety disorder. If you suspect your child may be struggling with an anxiety disorder, seeking guidance from a mental health professional is crucial.

  • Connecting with the Best Psychologists

    Education2.0's SchoolSense+ LMS/LXP understands the importance of connecting students and their families with the right mental health professionals. Through its partnership with MindShift, powered by One4patient EMR/EHR/LIS, SchoolSense+ provides access to a network of top-quality psychologists.

  • Powered by One4patient EMR/EHR/LIS

    One4patient EMR/EHR/LIS is a leading electronic medical record (EMR), electronic health records (EHR), and laboratory information system (LIS) provider. Their partnership with MindShift ensures a seamless experience for users, allowing them to easily connect with the best psychologists who can provide the necessary support and interventions.

  • Thriving Academically with Comprehensive Support

    Education2.0's SchoolSense+ LMS/LXP ensures that students receive comprehensive support by prioritising mental well-being and offering access to top-quality mental health professionals. This holistic approach empowers students to overcome mental health challenges, enabling them to thrive academically and reach their full potential. If you suspect your child may be struggling with a learning disability or an anxiety disorder, don't hesitate to explore the mental health monitoring tool, MindShift, within Education2.0's SchoolSense+ LMS/LXP. Seek guidance from a mental health professional and use the platform's partnership with MindShift, powered by One4patient EMR/EHR/LIS, to connect with the best psychologists who can provide the necessary support and care. Together, we can nurture students' mental well-being and create a positive learning environment.
